On the Subject of Letter Lock

  • The module will have a 4x4 grid with some squares being blacked out. The non-black squares will have a letter.
  • The letters are from a word that is from six to ten letters long, although the word may be shifted forward in the alphabet (Caesar Cipher forward.) These words can be found on the following page.
  • Decrypt the word and place each letter in the corresponding spot in reading order. The arrows on each column/row of the grid will shift each letter in that direction, looping over when necessary. Letters do not shift past black squares.
  • When a letter is in its correct position, press on the square to lock it into place. Whenever a letter is locked, it acts as if it were a black square. The word will be submitted whenever all letters are locked into place.
